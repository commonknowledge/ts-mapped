Zetkin is an organising tool widely used by political parties and campaign groups. Connecting it to Mapped lets you visualise your members and activists on a map.

## Before you start

You'll need a Zetkin account with API access. Contact your Zetkin organisation administrator if you're not sure whether API access is enabled.

## Connecting your account

### 1. Create a new data source

Go to **Data Sources**, click **New data source**, and select **Zetkin**.

### 2. Authenticate

Click **Connect Zetkin** and sign in to your Zetkin account when prompted. Grant Mapped read access to your organisation's data.

### 3. Select what to import

Choose the Zetkin resource you want to map — typically people (members) from your organisation.

### 4. Map your location fields

Select the fields that contain your members' address data. Mapped will geocode these to place them on the map.

### 5. Save and sync

Name your data source and click **Save**. Mapped will sync your Zetkin data automatically on a regular schedule, keeping your map up to date as new members are added or addresses change.
