Connecting Mailchimp lets you map your subscriber list geographically — useful for visualising where your audience is concentrated or planning local outreach.

## Before you start

You'll need a Mailchimp account with at least one audience (list) that contains address or postcode data for your subscribers.

## Connecting your account

### 1. Create a new data source

Go to **Data Sources**, click **New data source**, and select **Mailchimp**.

### 2. Authenticate

Click **Connect Mailchimp** and sign in to your Mailchimp account when prompted. Grant Mapped permission to read your audience data.

### 3. Select an audience

Choose the Mailchimp audience you want to import from the dropdown.

### 4. Map your location fields

Select the merge field that contains your subscribers' location data — typically a postcode or address field you've added to your signup form.

### 5. Save

Name your data source and click **Save**. Mapped will import your subscribers and geocode their locations.

## Keeping data in sync

Mapped will sync your Mailchimp audience periodically, picking up new subscribers and any updated address information automatically.
