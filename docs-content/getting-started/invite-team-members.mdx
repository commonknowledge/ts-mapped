Mapped is built around organisations — every map, data source, and layer belongs to an organisation, and multiple people can work within the same one. Inviting a colleague gives them access to your maps and data sources without needing to share credentials.

## Inviting someone

### 1. Open your organisation settings

Click your organisation name in the top navigation, then select **Settings**.

### 2. Go to the Members tab

Select the **Members** tab to see everyone currently in your organisation.

### 3. Send an invitation

Click **Invite member** and enter the person's email address. They'll receive an email with a link to accept the invitation and create their account.

### 4. They accept the invite

When they click the link in the email, they'll be prompted to set a password and are immediately added to your organisation with access to all its maps and data sources.

## Managing members

From the Members tab you can see all active members and any pending invitations. Pending invites can be resent or cancelled if the link has expired.
